🔒 Privacy Clause

At Elevate Software Company, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and handling all data with transparency, care, and respect.

📋 What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary for employment, project management, and providing you with the best possible workplace experience. This may include your contact details, identification documents, and employment records.

⚙️ How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely for business purposes—such as payroll, benefits, compliance, and internal communications. We do not share your personal data with unauthorized third parties.

🔐 Security & Confidentiality

We use secure systems and strict access controls to safeguard your data. Only authorized personnel can view or process your information, and all team members are trained in data privacy best practices.

🧾 Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. If you have questions or concerns about your data, you can reach out to our privacy team at privacy@elevatesoftware.com.

🔄 Continuous Commitment

We regularly review and update our privacy practices to ensure your information stays safe and confidential—because your trust matters to us.

At Elevate, your privacy is never an afterthought—it’s a promise.
